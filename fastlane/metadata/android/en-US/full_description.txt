An app that manages your phones volume. It will mute your phone for you, so that you never get disturbed when doing important things!

<b>Time</b>
Create schedules for when your phone should be muted or loud

<b>Calendar</b>
Let your calendar manage the volume for you. Depending on events, your phone will be silent!

<b>Keywords</b>
If you have specific events, keywords will help you get the silence you need!

<b>Bluetooth</b>
When a bluetooth device connects, your phone can react to it!

<b>Sanity Check</b>
Just so that you don't miss anything important, we have a checkup that you can use to verify your settings!
